WHO WE ARE

Tech Solutions is a signage and wayfinding consultancy with over ten year's team experience. We believe that wayfinding design starts and ends with user experience, that the function of wayfinding design is to help users navigate simply and in the right direction. We create design that integrate wayfinding with the context, both in terms of designs, landscape, digital information, architecture and other visual cues.

Our company is based in both Sharjah and Dubai and works all over the GCC, our team has experience in multiple sectors including healthcare, academic, retail and both master planned and mixed use residential developments.

What We Do

Research

We plan by studying and investing in research of user behavior and navigational requirements.

Strategy

Our strategy focuses on a holistic approach, incorporating broader considerations both in terms of human behavior and other factors such as vehicular traffic flows.

Concept Design

We study each project independently, incorporating and expressing the brand identity and values into each aspect of wayfinding design.

Schematic & Detailed Design

Using cutting edge technology, our design team graphically design the entire range of deliverables based on the approved concept, including family of sign, location, pictograms and typeface.

Implementation

We have worked with several trusted fabricators on material selection, industrial design production and installation.

Why Choose TSME?

Choose Tech Solutions for expert wayfinding design that prioritizes user experience, ensuring seamless navigation through intuitive, context-aware signage. With over a decade of experience across healthcare, retail, academic, and residential projects, our team delivers tailored solutions that blend design, architecture, and digital integration. Based in Sharjah and Dubai, we serve the entire GCC with innovative, functional wayfinding systems that enhance any environment.
